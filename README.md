# ECommerce-Sales-EDA

# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Analysis | Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the **DecodeLabs Data Analytics Internship Program**. The objective was to perform **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on an E-Commerce Sales Dataset to uncover business insights, identify trends, detect outliers, and support data-driven decision-making.

The analysis focuses on customer purchasing behavior, product performance, payment preferences, marketing effectiveness, and revenue trends using Python and data visualization techniques.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Understand dataset structure and quality
* Perform descriptive statistical analysis
* Identify trends and patterns in sales data
* Detect outliers and anomalies
* Analyze customer behavior
* Evaluate product performance
* Assess marketing channel effectiveness
* Generate actionable business insights

## 📊 Key Findings

### Product Performance

* 🏆 Printer is the most frequently purchased product.
* 💰 Chair generates the highest revenue.
* 📦 Laptop and Tablet contribute significantly to overall sales.

### Customer Behavior

* Customers purchase approximately 3 products per transaction.
* Average order value is approximately ₹1,054.

### Marketing Analysis

* 📱 Instagram is the highest-performing referral source.
* 📧 Email Marketing and Google Search are important acquisition channels.

### Payment Analysis

* 💳 Online payments are the most preferred transaction method.
* Payment distribution is relatively balanced across methods.

### Coupon Analysis

* 🎟️ FREESHIP is the most successful promotional campaign.
* Around 26% of customers completed purchases without coupons.

### Revenue Trends

* 📈 Revenue shows consistent growth during 2025.
* Significant increase observed from April to June 2025.

### Operational Insights

* ⚠️ High cancellation and return rates indicate opportunities for process improvement.
* Order fulfillment performance should be monitored closely.

## 🔍 Business Insights

1. Product demand is concentrated among Printers, Tablets, and Chairs.
2. Instagram is the most effective marketing channel.
3. Customers strongly respond to free shipping incentives.
4. Revenue growth indicates positive business momentum.
5. Order cancellations and returns require operational investigation.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

### Marketing

* Increase investment in Instagram campaigns.
* Expand successful coupon strategies similar to FREESHIP.

### Operations

* Investigate causes of order cancellations and returns.
* Improve order fulfillment efficiency.

### Sales Strategy

* Promote high-revenue products.
* Implement targeted campaigns for underperforming categories.
